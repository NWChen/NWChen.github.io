High-quality grappling content

2021/02/14

This is a collection of grappling (jiu-jitsu/wrestling/judo) content that seems consistently high signal-to-noise to me.

  • Ayrshire Grappler - text-only commentary of famous jiu-jitsu matches, with east coast hip-hop classics in the background

  • The Grapple Club - technique tutorials by prominent modern competitors

  • William Abreu - stand-up/takedown technique

Obviously practicing more grappling is the best way to absorb this information, but during quarantine I’m enjoying these channels.
